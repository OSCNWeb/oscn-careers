**NOTICE OF JUDICIAL VACANCY**
 
The Judicial Nominating Commission seeks applicants to fill a vacancy for the position of Judge for the Oklahoma Court of Civil Appeals, District 4, Office 2. This vacancy is created by the retirement of the Honorable Larry Joplin.
 
To be appointed to the office of Judge of the Court of Civil Appeals, one must be a legal resident of the respective district at the time (s)he takes the oath of office and assumes the duties of office. Additionally, prior to appointment, such appointee shall have had a minimum of four yearsâ€™ experience in Oklahoma as a licensed practicing attorney, a judge of a court of record, or both.

Applications must be submitted to the Chairman of the JNC no later than 5:00 p.m., Friday, March 6, 2020.  Applications may be hand-delivered or mailed.
